                                                                                                The first snowflakes of the year, the beginning                                                                                                 of spring, the scent of the perfume of an old                                                                                                 love, a piece music you didn't hear for a long                                                                                                 time...

                                                                                                The melancholy, carried by these connections                                                                                                 between the present and moments that are gone                                                                                                 forever, is a great source of inspiration                                                                                                 for my work. I am looking for ways to fill                                                                                                 spaces with atmospheres that appeal to                                                                                                 imagination, spheres from uncommon situations.                                                                                                 Light, sound and scent help me with that.
